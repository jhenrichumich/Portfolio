Dietary Care

Diet Recommendations will be designed for each patient's goals. Medifast is one of the oldest programs used in the United States. It has been in use for over 30 years at John Hopkins' weight loss program. Over 1 million patients have been on Medifast and over 15,000 physicians have prescribed the program. The Medifast program is one that allows you a "5 & 1" program: 5 Medifast bars or meal replacement and 1 lean and green meal. Typical weight loss is 2-4 pounds per week. The beauty of this program is the ability to still participate in meals with your family or when you go out; that will be the time you can do your meat and vegetable or meat and salad. Medifast is the cheapest controlled dietary program in the country. A controlled dietary program has the greatest impact on a patient's Hypertension and Diabetes.
